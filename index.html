About Sai Aranya

Here’s your tropical green sanctuary in Nerkundram, Chennai. Ramaniyam Sai Aranya signifies a charming project developed by Ramaniyam Real Estates Pvt Ltd. The property covers 3.14 acres in a prime area, offering airy, grand, and wellness-focused houses. The homes are designed in 1, 2, and 3 BHK configurations that cater to all types of families and even individuals in the city. You choose your convenient floor plan from a diverse range of unit sizes, starting from 806 sq. ft. to 1773 sq. ft. This shows the flexibility in house selection. Variety not only creates distinction here but also gives you the liberty to choose your suitable spot. Families living here are from various walks of life, and living in such an environment lets you experience different cultures within the same address. The gated community has 488 families enjoying city life. You get access to 18 amenities that focus on whole-family wellness, are socially thoughtful, and make you eco-conscious. Together, members of Ramaniyam Sai Aranya in Nerkundram experience the dignity of belonging to a vibrant circle of like-minded families. Mutual respect and personal comfort coexist in the same place.
